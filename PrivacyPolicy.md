# Privacy Policy for ChordRain

**Last Updated: October 26, 2023**

Quitofilms ("we," "our," or "us") provides the **ChordRain** mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**ChordRain does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.** 

### Local Data Storage
The app stores your game progress and high scores locally on your device using "Web Storage" (localStorage). This data is stored solely on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## 2. Permissions
Our app may request the following permissions:
*   **Internet Access:** Used solely to support internal WebView functions. No user data is transmitted over the internet.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking software.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Because we do not collect any personal information, our app is safe for children. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
